About Me

Combining quantitative finance expertise with cutting-edge AI to solve complex problems at scale.

I am a Senior Data Scientist and Tech Lead at MDPI in Basel, Switzerland, where I lead Data Intelligence products and develop end-to-end AI solutions. My expertise spans LLM pipelines, GenAI, classification, clustering, causal inference, graph ML, time series forecasting, and vector database applications‚Äîall built in Python and deployed via Streamlit and Superset.

With a Master's in Economic Statistics specializing in Financial Markets and Insurance from the University of Milan, and a subsequent Master's in Data Science from University of Bicocca (2021), I combine strong quantitative foundations with practical machine learning expertise.
